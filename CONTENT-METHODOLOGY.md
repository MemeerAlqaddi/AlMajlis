# Content methodology

The Majlis separates answer-based games from conversation prompts.

- **Game Night Shuffle** contains only Guess the Word, Decode, Complete the Ayah, Trivia, and Riddles. These cards have a checkable answer and may be scored.
- **Conversation Shuffle** contains only Dilemmas and Under the Surface. These prompts are not scored and cannot be played as Teams or 1 vs 1.
- Dilemma responses are discussion considerations, not legal rulings or fatwas.
- Qur’an and hadith references are shown for checking after an answer is revealed. English text is a concise gameplay aid, not a replacement for tafsir, formal translation, or scholarship.
- Country-qualified dialect labels mean a phrase is common in that country, not unique to it. English sound spellings are approximate; the revealed Arabic is the reference.

This release must not be described as scholar-verified or native-dialect-reviewed until qualified reviewers are named and their review scope is documented.

Every card has a permanent ID. Existing IDs must never be reassigned, even when wording changes or a card is retired. Corrections should record the card ID, content version, old wording, new wording, reason, and reviewer.
