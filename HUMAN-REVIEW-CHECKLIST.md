# Human Review Checklist

Automated tests confirm structure; qualified human reviewers must confirm meaning and natural usage.

## Islamic reviewer

For every Trivia, Complete the Ayah, Riddle, Guess the Word, and Dilemma card:

- Confirm the displayed answer is accurate.
- Confirm the cited source supports the precise claim.
- Confirm Qur’anic Arabic and verse boundaries against an authoritative muṣḥaf source.
- Confirm hadith grading and numbering against the named collection.
- Mark interpretation, scholarly difference, or context that makes a single answer misleading.
- Confirm Dilemma wording cannot reasonably be mistaken for a fatwa.
- Record reviewer name, qualification, date, card ID, and approval or correction.

## Arabic reviewers

Use at least one native reviewer for each labeled variety and one cross-Levantine reviewer:

- Confirm the Arabic phrase is natural and correctly written.
- Confirm the sound-spelling reliably prompts the intended phrase.
- Confirm the regional label describes real usage without implying exclusivity.
- Re-label shared expressions as Levantine when country attribution is too narrow.
- Record reviewer name, variety, date, card ID, and approval or correction.

## Release sign-off

- No unresolved high-severity content reports.
- Every card has a named reviewer record.
- Corrections preserve the permanent card ID.
- `contentVersion` is advanced after approved content changes.
- Reviewer credits and the review date are added to the public content notes.
