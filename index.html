HOW THE CONTENT WORKS

Clear rules. Honest limits.

Knowledge games have a checkable answer and can be scored. Dilemmas and Under the Surface are conversation prompts, never right-or-wrong contests.

Islamic sources are shown only after Reveal. Qur’an and hadith references are included for checking; English wording is a concise gameplay aid, not a replacement for tafsir or a formal translation.

Dilemmas are not fatwas. Their considerations are starting points. Personal rulings belong with a qualified scholar who knows the situation.

Arabic labels describe usage, not ownership. “Common in Syria,” for example, does not mean a phrase is exclusive to Syria. Sound spellings are approximate and the revealed Arabic is the reference.

This release has not been certified as scholar-reviewed or native-dialect-reviewed. Please report anything unclear or inaccurate.
